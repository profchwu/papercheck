NTHU Sustainability Paper Checker

Developed by Professor Chih-Hung Wu, Institute of Mathematics and Science Education, National Tsing Hua University.

A browser-based SDG detectability checker for title, abstract, and keywords.

This tool is for reference only. PASS/FAIL reflects this tool's rule-based assessment using downloaded SDG mapping references; the final recognition decision must follow National Tsing Hua University's official review and standards.

Paper Input

Paste all fields together, then parse or edit each field manually.

Assessment

Best-fit SDG and rule-based recognition result.

Run a check to see PASS/FAIL, best-fit SDG, matched terms, and revision advice.

Marked Review

Red marks show text that should be revised. Green marks show matched SDG terms.

Marked title, abstract, and keywords will appear here.
